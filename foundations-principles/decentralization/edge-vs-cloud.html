Edge vs Cloud

Favor local control over centralized hosting.

Why It Matters

Running nodes on personal hardware reduces reliance on major cloud providers.

Key Concepts

  • Edge nodes improve censorship resistance
  • Local infrastructure avoids single-provider outages
  • Community hosting fosters grassroots participation

Examples / Projects

  • Home-hosted Ethereum validators
  • Mesh networks for local block propagation

Challenges & Risks

  • Higher maintenance burden for individuals
  • Residential bandwidth limitations

Trade-offs

Latency, UX, and costs can rise with distribution.

Why It Matters

Decentralization introduces overhead that impacts performance and usability.

Key Concepts

  • More nodes mean slower consensus
  • Extra redundancy increases cost
  • Designs must balance convenience and resilience

Examples / Projects

  • Higher fees on decentralized networks
  • Centralized exchanges offering faster UX

Challenges & Risks

  • User impatience with slow confirmations
  • Pressure to recentralize for efficiency

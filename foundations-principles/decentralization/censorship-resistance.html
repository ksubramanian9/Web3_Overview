Censorship Resistance

Hard to block transactions or exclude users.

Why It Matters

Ensures anyone can submit transactions without gatekeepers.

Key Concepts

  • Validators include valid transactions regardless of origin
  • Economic incentives discourage filtering
  • Relies on distributed consensus rather than single operators

Examples / Projects

  • Bitcoin’s open mempool
  • MEV-Boost relays aiming for neutrality

Challenges & Risks

  • Regulatory pressure on block producers
  • Network-level filtering by ISPs

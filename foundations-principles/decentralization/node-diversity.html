Node Diversity

Many independent operators reduce capture risk.

Why It Matters

Diverse node operators make censorship or coordinated failure less likely.

Key Concepts

  • Different clients and geographies prevent single points of failure
  • Encourages open participation and resilience
  • Balances performance with decentralization goals

Examples / Projects

  • Ethereum’s multi-client ecosystem
  • Bitcoin’s global miner distribution

Challenges & Risks

  • Hardware cost for running full nodes
  • Centralized cloud hosting concentration

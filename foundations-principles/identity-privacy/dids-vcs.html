DIDs & VCs

Decentralized identifiers and verifiable credentials.

Why It Matters

They enable portable, self-owned identities verified by third parties.

Key Concepts

  • DIDs are unique identifiers without centralized registry
  • VCs contain signed attestations
  • Holders present proofs to verifiers

Examples / Projects

  • W3C DID spec implementations
  • Ethereum Name Service

Challenges & Risks

  • Interoperability between DID methods
  • Revocation and key rotation

Bridges

Move assets/messages across chains securely.

Why It Matters

Bridges connect isolated ecosystems for liquidity and data transfer.

Key Concepts

  • Lock-and-mint or burn-and-release mechanisms
  • Relayers or validators verify cross-chain events
  • Security varies with trust assumptions

Examples / Projects

  • Wormhole bridge
  • Axelar cross-chain network

Challenges & Risks

  • Bridge hacks from compromised validators
  • Liquidity fragmentation

Governance

Token-based voting lets communities steer protocol parameters and upgrades.

Why It Matters

Governance mechanisms align stakeholder interests and provide a transparent way to evolve decentralized systems.

Key Concepts

  • Token-weighted voting and proposal thresholds
  • Delegation to active representatives
  • Quorum and time-lock protections
  • On-chain versus off-chain decision processes

Examples / Projects

  • Compound DAO managing interest rate models
  • Aave governance setting collateral parameters

Challenges & Risks

  • Voter apathy concentrates power in few hands
  • Plutocratic influence from large token holders
  • Governance attacks exploiting low participation

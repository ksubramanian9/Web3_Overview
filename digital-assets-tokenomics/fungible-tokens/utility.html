Utility

Fungible tokens often pay fees, unlock services, or reward desired behavior.

Why It Matters

Useful tokens align network usage with value creation and drive sustainable economies.

Key Concepts

  • Gas tokens that pay for computation
  • Access tokens granting service usage
  • Reward tokens incentivizing participation
  • Staking tokens securing a network

Examples / Projects

  • ETH used for gas fees on Ethereum
  • BNB providing discounts on Binance
  • FIL paying for decentralized storage on Filecoin

Challenges & Risks

  • Speculation can overshadow actual utility
  • Demand for services may fluctuate widely
  • Unclear regulatory treatment for some utilities

Liquidity

Pools and markets allow tokens to trade quickly with minimal price impact.

Why It Matters

Deep liquidity attracts traders and makes a token useful for payments, collateral, and other integrations.

Key Concepts

  • Automated market makers versus order books
  • Liquidity providers earning swap fees
  • Slippage and price impact for large trades
  • Impermanent loss when prices diverge

Examples / Projects

  • Uniswap AMM pools for ERC-20 tokens
  • Curve Finance optimized for stablecoins
  • Balancer multi-asset liquidity pools

Challenges & Risks

  • Impermanent loss can outweigh fees for LPs
  • Liquidity fragmentation across chains and venues
  • Regulatory uncertainty for market makers

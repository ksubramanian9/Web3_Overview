Compliance

Regulatory controls like whitelists and disclosures help tokens meet legal obligations.

Why It Matters

Compliance features enable institutions to participate while reducing legal and reputational risk.

Key Concepts

  • KYC/AML checks for token issuers and users
  • Whitelist and blacklist mechanisms
  • Travel Rule data sharing for transfers
  • On-chain disclosures for regulated assets

Examples / Projects

  • USDC and USDT address freezing to enforce sanctions
  • Chainalysis KYT monitoring suspicious flows

Challenges & Risks

  • Overcompliance can erode censorship resistance
  • Jurisdictional rules differ globally
  • Implementing controls adds operational cost

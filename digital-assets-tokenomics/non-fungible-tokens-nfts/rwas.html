RWAs

Tokenized real-world assets bring physical value and rights on-chain.

Why It Matters

Digitizing assets enables fractional ownership, faster settlement, and access to global liquidity.

Key Concepts

  • Legal wrappers and custodians hold the underlying asset
  • Oracles attest to off-chain ownership and valuations
  • Compliance with securities and property laws is essential

Examples / Projects

  • RealT fractionalizes rental properties as NFTs
  • MakerDAO uses Centrifuge to onboard real estate and treasury bills

Challenges & Risks

  • Regulatory uncertainty across jurisdictions
  • Custodial arrangements introduce counterparty risk

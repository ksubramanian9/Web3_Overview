Royalties

Royalties return a portion of secondary sales to the original creators.

Why It Matters

Recurring revenue helps artists and teams sustain development while aligning incentives with holders.

Key Concepts

  • Marketplace-level versus on-chain royalty enforcement
  • ERC-2981 standard communicates royalty fee and recipient
  • Split contracts automatically distribute proceeds

Examples / Projects

  • OpenSea allows creators to set secondary sale fees
  • Manifold and Zora implement on-chain royalty splits

Challenges & Risks

  • Marketplaces can bypass royalty payments to attract traders
  • Legal recognition and enforcement remain uncertain

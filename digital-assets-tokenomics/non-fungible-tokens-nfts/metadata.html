Metadata

Metadata defines an NFT's traits, provenance, and media references.

Why It Matters

Where and how metadata is stored determines the permanence and trustworthiness of NFT attributes.

Key Concepts

  • On-chain versus off-chain metadata storage
  • Decentralized hosting via IPFS or Arweave
  • Immutable vs. mutable metadata updates

Examples / Projects

  • CryptoPunks store all metadata and art directly on-chain
  • Bored Ape Yacht Club serves JSON traits from IPFS

Challenges & Risks

  • Centralized servers can disappear or alter content
  • Mutable metadata may change an NFT's value after purchase

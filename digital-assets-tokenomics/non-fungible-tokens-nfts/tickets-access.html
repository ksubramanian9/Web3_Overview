Tickets/Access

NFTs can act as verifiable tickets or memberships with programmable perks.

Why It Matters

Tokenized access reduces fraud, enables secondary markets, and lets issuers add utility over time.

Key Concepts

  • Token gating grants entry to events or communities
  • Time-bound or revocable passes manage transferability
  • Secondary markets can embed royalties or perks

Examples / Projects

  • POAP badges certify event attendance
  • Ticketmaster pilots NFT ticketing on Polygon

Challenges & Risks

  • Scalping and bot purchases still require mitigation
  • Lost wallets can lock users out of access

PoS Staking

Validators bond tokens and get slashed for faults.

Why It Matters

Proof-of-stake secures networks with economic penalties instead of energy use.

Key Concepts

  • Validators lock stake to participate
  • Rewards incentivize honest behavior
  • Slashing deters double-signing and downtime

Examples / Projects

  • Ethereum's Beacon Chain
  • Cosmos staking

Challenges & Risks

  • Capital requirement centralizes validators
  • Complex withdrawal mechanics
